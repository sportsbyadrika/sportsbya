Shooting Sports Suite
shootingsports.in

Purpose-built for rifle, pistol, and shotgun events with compliant scoring and deep analytics.

  • Scoring workflows with validations for ISSF/NRAI formats.
  • Automated reports, certificates, and medal tallies.

  • Performance analytics: shot charts, group size, trends.
